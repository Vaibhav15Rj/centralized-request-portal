PowerGrid - Company Overview

Who We Are

POWERGRID is a Maharatna Public Sector Enterprise of the Govt. of India, incorporated on 23rd October 1989. With a holding of 51.34% by the Government and the balance by institutional/public investors, it is India's largest electric power transmission utility.
